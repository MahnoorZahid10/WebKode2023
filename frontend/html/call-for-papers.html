    • GUIDELINES FOR AUTHORS

    PAPER FORMATTING INSTRUCTIONS
  • SUBMITTED PAPERS SHOULD PREFERABLY BE 8-10 PAGES IN LENGTH INCLUDING TABLES, FIGURES AND REFERENCES
  • PAPER LENGTH: 3 PAGES + 1 PAGE FOR REFERENCES. THE PAPER LENGTH SHOULD NOT EXCEED 4 DOUBLE-COLUMN PAGES. FURTHER, THE LAST PAGE OF PAPER SHOULD HAVE REFERENCES ONLY.
  • PAPER SIZE: US LETTER (8.5" X 11") OR A4 (210MM X 297MM)
  • FONTS: EMBED ALL FONTS IN YOUR PDF FILE. AVOID THE USE OF TYPE 3 FONTS.
  • FILE FORMAT: ADOBE PDF (.PDF)
  • ALLOWED FILE SIZE: 5.0MB
  • DO NOT PAGE NUMBER YOUR PAPER
  • DO NOT APPLY SECURITY SETTINGS TO YOUR PDF FILE.

    • SUBMISSION PROCESS

    SUBMISSION INSTRUCTIONS
  • SUBMITTED PAPER MUST BE ORIGINAL, UNPUBLISHED, AND NOT SUBMITTED TO ANOTHER CONFERENCE OR JOURNAL FOR CONSIDERATION.
  • PAPERS MUST BE SUBMITTED ELECTRONICALLY THROUGH ICONICS EASY CHAIR SUBMISSION SYSTEM
  • EACH SUBMITTED PAPER WILL UNDERGO A PEER REVIEW PROCESS.
  • PLEASE CONTACT paper@nedieee.com IF YOU HAVE ANY QUESTIONS OR DIFFICULTY WITH YOUR PAPER SUBMISSION.
